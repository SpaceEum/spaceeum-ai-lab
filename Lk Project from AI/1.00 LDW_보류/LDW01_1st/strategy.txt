**Trading Strategy: Candlestick Comparison**

**Objective:** Analyze recent candlestick data to determine buying, selling or holding behavior based on price and volume patterns.

**Data definition (using Pandas DataFrame 'df'):**:

* ** Previous closing price: ** 'df['shut up'.iloc[-1]]' (last completed candle closing price)
* **Previous lows: ** 'min(df['open']).iloc[-1], df['closed'.iloc[-1]]' (low value between last completed candle opening and closing)
* **Previous high (before two cycles): ** 'max(df['open'.iloc[-2]], df['closed'.iloc[-2]]')' (high value between candle opening  before two cycles)
* ** Previous lows (before two cycles): ** 'min(df['open'.iloc[-2]], df['closed'.iloc[-2]]')' (low value between candle opening and closing before two cycles)
* **Previous volume:** 'df['volume'.iloc[-1]]'
* ** Average volume (21 periods): ** 'df['volume'] average for the last 21 periods (currently excluding incomplete candles).
* ** Candle's closing price will be above the 20-day moving average.
* **The paper price will rise on the 20th (the paper price on the 20th compares the current closing price with the previous closing price on the 20th, and the statement that the paper price rises means the current closing price is larger than the previous closing price on the 20th. Conversely, a drop in the paper price means the current closing price is lower than the previous closing price on the 20th)
* ** Close should be higher than prior span 1.
* **It should be higher than the lowest position among leading span 1[-1] to leading span 1[-26]

**Terms of Purchase:**:

1. **Price condition: ** Buy signal will be generated in the following cases:
* 'Previous closing price' > 'Previous high (before 2 cycles)'
2. **Check q'ty (signal strengthening): **If price conditions are met, *consider strengthening purchase signals and placing orders*:
* 'Previous Volume' >= ('Average Volume (21 Duration)" * 1.3)
* * (Note: Multiplier 1.3 is adjustable.)*

**Terms of Sale:**:

* **Initial stop loss:**:
* Set the initial stop loss price at purchase to 'Previous low (before 2 periods).
* Sell when the current price falls below this initial stop loss level.
* **Trailing stop-loss:**:
* **Update rules: ** Calculate potential new stop loss levels once new candles are complete: 'Previous low'
* If this 'previous low' is *higher than the current active stop loss level (initially or previously updated trailing stop), update the active stop loss to this new 'previous low'.
* If this 'previous low' is *below or equal to the current active stop loss level, do not change the current active stop loss level.
* **If current price falls below *active* stop loss level, we sell Sales Trigger 1:** (may be initial stop loss or latest trailing stop loss).
* **Sales trigger 2:** Sell if current price falls more than 5% from the highest closing price recorded since opening ('df['Close']'*.

**Pending conditions:**:

* ** Holding position: ** If you currently hold assets, you will continue to hold them if the selling conditions are not met.
* **Purchase Holds: ** If you do not hold assets, you will hold (not buy) the purchase if the purchase conditions (especially the price conditions) are not met.

**Trading Strategy: Candlestick Comparison**

**Objective:** Analyze recent candlestick data to determine buy, sell, or hold actions based on price and volume patterns.

**Data Definitions (using Pandas DataFrame `df`):**

*   **Previous Close:** `df['close'].iloc[-1]` (Closing price of the last completed candle)
*   **Previous Low Point:** `min(df['open'].iloc[-1], df['close'].iloc[-1])` (The lower value between the open and close of the last completed candle)
*   **Prior High Point (2 periods ago):** `max(df['open'].iloc[-2], df['close'].iloc[-2])` (The higher value between the open and close of the candle from two periods ago)
*   **Prior Low Point (2 periods ago):** `min(df['open'].iloc[-2], df['close'].iloc[-2])` (The lower value between the open and close of the candle from two periods ago)
*   **Previous Volume:** `df['volume'].iloc[-1]`
*   **Average Volume (21 periods):** The average of `df['volume']` over the last 21 periods (excluding the current, incomplete candle).

**Buy Conditions:**

1.  **Price Condition:** A buy signal is generated if:
    *   `Previous Close` > `Prior High Point (2 periods ago)`
2.  **Volume Confirmation (Signal Reinforcement):** If the Price Condition is met, *consider* reinforcing the buy signal and placing an order if:
    *   `Previous Volume` >= (`Average Volume (21 periods)` * 1.3)
    *   *(Note: The multiplier 1.3 is adjustable.)*

**Sell Conditions:**

*   **Initial Stop-Loss:**
    *   Set the initial stop-loss price to `Prior Low Point (2 periods ago)` *at the time of purchase*.
    *   Sell if the current price drops below this initial stop-loss level.
*   **Trailing Stop-Loss:**
    *   **Update Rule:** When a new candle completes, calculate a potential new stop-loss level: `Previous Low Point`.
    *   If this `Previous Low Point` is *higher* than the current active stop-loss level (whether it's the initial or a previously updated trailing stop), update the active stop-loss to this new `Previous Low Point`.
    *   If this `Previous Low Point` is *lower* than or equal to the current active stop-loss level, keep the current active stop-loss level unchanged.
    *   **Sell Trigger 1:** Sell if the current price drops below the *active* stop-loss level (which could be the initial stop-loss or the latest trailing stop-loss).
    *   **Sell Trigger 2:** Sell if the current price drops more than 5% below the highest closing price (`df['close']`) recorded *since the position was opened*.

**Hold Conditions:**

*   **Hold Position:** If currently holding an asset, continue to hold if none of the Sell Conditions are met.
*   **Hold Off Buying:** If not holding an asset, hold off (do not buy) if the Buy Conditions (specifically the Price Condition) are not met.

**Commissions:**

*   A commission fee of 0.0005% (0.000005) of the transaction value applies to both buy and sell orders. This should be factored into profit/loss calculations in a live trading implementation.
